ASSIMILATION

Merge with the boundless expanse of the void, welcome the 0blivion that lies in wait, for it is within this acceptance that you can find solace from the animosity, the sickening, ever-present static noise that resonates through every bone in your body. allow yourself to gaze into the precipice, cut out the noise, it is only you and your mind, assimilate into 0blivion and permeate every iota of the cosmos.
